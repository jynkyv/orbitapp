## FAQ

### How do I sync with Apple Reminders?
Go to **Settings > Connected Apps > Reminders** and tap "Sync with Reminders". Select the lists you want to sync with Orbit.

### How do I connect my Obsidian vault?
Go to **Settings > Connected Apps** and select your Obsidian vault folder. Orbit will scan for todos in your markdown files.

### Is my data private?
Yes! Orbit does not collect any personal data. All your tasks are stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

### What is Orbit Pro?
Orbit Pro unlocks premium features including:
- iCloud sync across all your Apple devices
- Home screen widgets
- Focus Mode for deep work sessions
- Priority support

### How do I restore my purchase?
Go to **Settings** and tap "Restore Purchases" in the Orbit Pro section.

### Which iOS versions are supported?
Orbit requires iOS 17.0 or later.
