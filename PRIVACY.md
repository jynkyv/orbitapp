# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 30, 2026**

## Introduction

SyncInsect ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Orbit mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Task Data**: Tasks, lists, folders, and other content you create within the App
- **Preferences**: App settings and configuration choices

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect personal identification information
- We do **not** track your location
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking services
- We do **not** display advertisements
- We do **not** sell or share your data with third parties

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
By default, all your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

### iCloud Sync (Optional)
If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is:
- Encrypted by Apple
- Accessible only to you
- Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

### Apple Reminders Integration (Optional)
If you connect Apple Reminders, the App accesses your Reminders data through Apple's APIs. This data remains within Apple's ecosystem and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

### Obsidian Vault Sync (Optional)
If you connect an Obsidian vault, the App reads and writes to files in the folder you specify. This data remains on your device or in your chosen sync service (iCloud, Dropbox, etc.).

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services
The App uses the following Apple services:
- **StoreKit**: For in-app purchases (Orbit Pro)
- **iCloud**: For optional data sync
- **EventKit**: For Apple Reminders integration

These services are subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:
- All data remains on your device unless you explicitly enable sync features
- iCloud data is encrypted by Apple
- We do not transmit your data to our servers

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
